## 🌈 **Beautiful Gradients**

### Featured Gradients:
1. **Primary Gradient**: Navy → Blue
   - `linear-gradient(135deg, #0A1F44 0%, #2563EB 100%)`
   - Used for header and primary buttons

2. **Secondary Gradient**: Purple → Pink
   - `linear-gradient(135deg, #7C3AED 0%, #EC4899 100%)`
   - Used for secondary buttons and forms

3. **Accent Gradient**: Cyan → Sky Blue
   - `linear-gradient(135deg, #06B6D4 0%, #0EA5E9 100%)`
   - Used for highlights and special elements

4. **Rainbow Gradient**: Navy → Purple → Pink
   - `linear-gradient(135deg, #0A1F44 0%, #7C3AED 50%, #EC4899 100%)`
   - Used for section titles and headings

5. **Light Gradient**: Light Blue → Light Purple
   - `linear-gradient(135deg, #F0F9FF 0%, #F5F3FF 100%)`
   - Used for page backgrounds
